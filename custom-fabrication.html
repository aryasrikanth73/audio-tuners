About We

With over 18 years of experience in the 12v audio industry, Mr. Imthiyaz is the driving force behind AudioTunerz. As the owner and chief tuner, he combines technical expertise with a deep passion for car audio, ensuring that every project meets the highest standards of quality and performance. His advanced training at Indonesia's Audioworkshop "Top Gun Tuning Camp" and his role as an EMMA-trained judge are testaments to his exceptional skills and dedication to the craft.


Mr. Imthiyaz has been recognized with multiple EMMA awards, a testament to his ability to deliver outstanding audio experiences. But what truly sets him apart is his commitment to listening to his customers. He understands that every client has unique tastes and budgets, and he works closely with each one to create custom audio solutions that align perfectly with their vision.


At AudioTunerz, Mr. Imthiyaz's leadership has established a reputation for delivering audio installations that not only sound incredible but also seamlessly integrate with the aesthetics of each vehicle. His attention to detail and willingness to tailor his services to each client's specific needs make him the go-to expert for those seeking the ultimate in car audio performance.

Turn up the volume and let your ride become a symphony of sound.

At Audio Tunerz Car Audio, we specialize in delivering high-performance sound systems that transform your driving experience. With top-tier products and expert installation, we bring clarity, power, and precision to your vehicle’s audio. Trust us to elevate your in-car entertainment with innovative solutions tailored to your needs.
